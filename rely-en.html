FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can we make small changes on the day?

Absolutely. If time and the overall flow of the day allow, I’ll do my best to adjust things naturally without disrupting the experience.

What if we prefer a slower pace?

That’s completely fine. The schedule can be adjusted around your energy and comfort. I care more about how the day feels than about rushing through a checklist.

What happens if the weather or local conditions change?

I’ll adjust the order of the day and shift priorities as needed, aiming to keep the experience smooth while making the most of the time you have.
